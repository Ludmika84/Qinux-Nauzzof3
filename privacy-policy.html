Privacy policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ECOMM MOVADGENCY SL (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses and protects personal data when you visit this Qinux Nauzzof promotional website directed at consumers in the United Kingdom.

We process personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 where applicable, and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR).

1. Data controller

The data controller for this website is:

  • Organisation: ECOMM MOVADGENCY SL
  • Email: support@qinux-nauzzof.co.uk
  • Contact form: Contact us

If you complete a purchase on an external retailer or checkout website after clicking an order link, that retailer is an independent controller for the personal data processed for your order, payment and fulfilment. Their privacy notice will apply to that processing.

2. Personal data we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may process:

  • Identity and contact data — name, email address and any information you include in a message submitted via our contact form
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, time zone setting and approximate location derived from IP
  • Usage data — pages viewed, links clicked, referring URL, landing-page parameters (including advertising or affiliate campaign IDs) and session interactions
  • Consent and preference data — your cookie consent choice and related preference records
  • Communications data — records of enquiries and our responses

We do not intentionally collect special category data (such as health data) through this website. Please do not include sensitive medical details in contact messages unless necessary.

3. How we collect data

  • Directly from you when you submit a form or email us
  • Automatically via cookies, pixels, local storage and similar technologies (see our Cookie policy)
  • From advertising or affiliate partners (for example campaign identifiers passed in URLs when you arrive from Taboola or similar networks)

4. Purposes and lawful bases

Under UK GDPR we only process personal data where we have a lawful basis:

  • Responding to enquiries and support — performance of a contract or steps prior to a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)), or legitimate interests in assisting users (Art. 6(1)(f))
  • Operating, securing and improving the website — legitimate interests in providing a secure and functional service (Art. 6(1)(f)); strictly necessary cookies may also rely on PECR exemptions
  • Remembering cookie preferences — consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) and/or legitimate interests in honouring your choice
  • Analytics and advertising measurement (including campaign performance for networks such as Taboola) — consent where required by PECR/UK GDPR (Art. 6(1)(a)); where a PECR exemption applies for limited statistical purposes under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025, we will still provide clear information and an easy way to object
  • Legal and regulatory compliance — legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))
  • Establishing, exercising or defending legal claims — legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f))

Where we rely on legitimate interests, we balance those interests against your rights and freedoms. You may object to processing based on legitimate interests as explained below.

5. Marketing and electronic communications

We do not send marketing emails or SMS from this website unless you have given appropriate PECR consent (or another PECR-compliant basis applies). If you later opt in on an external retailer site, that retailer’s marketing rules apply. You can withdraw marketing consent at any time using the unsubscribe method provided or by contacting us.

6. Sharing your data

We may share personal data with:

  • Hosting, IT, security and email service providers acting as processors under written contracts
  • Analytics and advertising partners where you have consented to non-essential cookies/technologies, or where a lawful PECR exemption applies
  • External retailers or offer platforms when you choose to continue to checkout
  • Professional advisers (lawyers, accountants) and authorities where required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal data. Processors may only use your data on our instructions and must keep it secure.

7. International transfers

Some service providers or advertising partners may process data outside the United Kingdom. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards under UK GDPR, such as:

  • a UK adequacy regulation; or
  • the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) / Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses; or
  • another lawful transfer mechanism recognised under UK law

You may request more information about transfer safeguards by contacting us.

8. Retention

  • Contact form enquiries: normally up to 24 months after the last meaningful correspondence, unless a longer period is needed for an ongoing matter
  • Cookie consent records: for the life of the consent preference (or until you clear site data), typically up to 12 months before we ask again
  • Server/security logs: typically 30–90 days, unless needed longer for security investigations
  • Legal claims / compliance records: for the applicable limitation period or as required by law

When data is no longer needed, we delete or irreversibly anonymise it.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse or alteration. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure; please take care when sharing information online.

10. Children’s data

This website is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it. Product suitability for younger travellers should be assessed by a parent or guardian; purchases are completed under the retailer’s terms.

11. Automated decision-making

We do not use solely automated decision-making (including profiling) that produces legal or similarly significant effects about you on this promotional website.

12. Your rights under UK GDPR

Subject to legal limits, you have the right to:

  • be informed about how we use your data (this policy)
  • access your personal data
  • rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • erase data in certain circumstances (“right to be forgotten”)
  • restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • data portability (where processing is based on consent or contract and is automated)
  • object to processing based on legitimate interests, and to direct marketing
  • withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent (without affecting prior lawful processing)
  • not be subject to solely automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects

To exercise your rights, email support@qinux-nauzzof.co.uk or use our Contact page. We may need to verify your identity. We aim to respond within one month, or as otherwise required by UK law.

13. Complaints

If you have a concern about how we handle your personal data, please contact us first so we can try to resolve it. Under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 we maintain a data protection complaints process and will acknowledge and handle complaints in line with applicable requirements.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK supervisory authority:

  • Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)
  • Website: https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/
  • Helpline: 0303 123 1113

14. External websites and offers

Order / offer buttons may redirect you to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please read their privacy notices before providing payment or delivery details.

15. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Significant changes may also be highlighted on the website.

16. Contact

Privacy questions: support@qinux-nauzzof.co.uk or Contact us.
